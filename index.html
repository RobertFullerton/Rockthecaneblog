About Jen

Jen, with a Masters in Special Education from California State University, has been an Orientation and Mobility Specialist for 12 years and practices in the greater San Diego area. She is experienced in working with children 3 trough 22 years old, and many of her students have multiple disabilities. Jen has a passion for empowering her students to be as independent as possible in every area of their lives. Jen started the “Rock the Cane” campaign to change the world’s perception of vision loss through the power of collaboration.
